## **Thank You for Testing the First Prototype!**

I appreciate your Feedback

If you encounter any bugs, have suggestions for improvements, or want to share your overall experience, please create a feedback ticket using the link below:

👉 https://github.com/itzmaiku44/Giftable.GG/issues/new

To make it easier for us to understand and address your feedback, consider including:

- **$\color{blue}{\text{Title:}}$** Bug Report, Feature Request, UI/UX Suggestion, or General Feedback
- **$\color{blue}{\text{Description:}}$** A clear explanation of what you experienced.
- **$\color{blue}{\text{Steps to Reproduce:}}$** (For bugs) What actions led to the issue?
- **$\color{blue}{\text{Expected Behavior:}}$** What did you expect to happen?
- **$\color{blue}{\text{Actual Behavior:}}$** What actually happened?
- **$\color{blue}{\text{Screenshots or Screen Recordings:}}$** If applicable.
- **$\color{blue}{\text{Browser and Device:}}$** (e.g., Chrome 138 on Windows 11, Safari on iPhone)
